About Us

Welcome to Sargam AI – a revolutionary project born from passion, innovation, and a shared love for Indian Classical Music. Developed during a high-stakes hackathon, our team of four talented developers combined AI, deep learning, and music theory to craft a unique AI-driven platform that redefines raga synthesis, analysis, and simulation.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between AI and Indian Classical Music, enabling musicians, researchers, and enthusiasts to explore music through the lens of technology. We strive to enhance musical creativity, foster learning, and preserve the rich heritage of ragas through AI.
