Frequently Asked Questions

What is Indian Classical Music Analysis?

Our AI analysis system analyzes the complex patterns, microtones, and melodic structures in Indian classical music, identifying ragas, taals, and other musical elements with high precision.

How does AI Synthesis work for Indian Classical Music?

Our AI synthesis models generate authentic Indian classical melodies using deep learning trained on thousands of traditional compositions, respecting raaga rules, melodic patterns, and traditional aesthetics.

Can I use Sargam AI to learn Indian Classical Music?

Absolutely! Sargam AI offers educational tools, interactive simulations, and personalized feedback to help students understand ragas, improve technique, and deepen their appreciation of classical traditions.

Is Sargam AI suitable for professional musicians?

Yes, many professional musicians use Sargam AI to explore new musical ideas, analyze their performances, and deepen their understanding of tradition. Our Professional tier includes advanced tools specifically designed for performers and composers.
